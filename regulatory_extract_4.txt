Regulatory Extract – Sample Document 4

Clause 10 – Data Protection
- Personal data must be encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Consent must be recorded for all data subjects.

Clause 15 – Record Keeping
- Records must be retained for a minimum of 7 years.
- Audit logs must be reviewed quarterly for anomalies.

Clause 20 – Reporting Obligations
- All incidents of non-compliance must be reported within 72 hours.
- Senior management must be informed of all material deficiencies.
